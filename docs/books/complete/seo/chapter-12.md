# 1. Introduction

E-E-A-T stands for **Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness**. It is a framework used by Google's Search Quality Rater Guidelines to evaluate the overall quality and credibility of content.

Although E-E-A-T is not a direct ranking factor, websites that consistently demonstrate these qualities are more likely to earn trust from users and perform well in search results.

---

# 2. Why E-E-A-T Matters

Strong E-E-A-T helps to:

- Build user trust
- Improve brand credibility
- Support higher-quality content
- Increase authority within a topic
- Strengthen visibility in AI-powered search
- Improve long-term SEO performance
